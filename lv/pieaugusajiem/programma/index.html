Programma uz zilo jostu

Tehnika, kas nepieciešams zilās jostas eksāmenam.
Progress ir individuāls un atkarīgs no ieguldītā darba, tomēr vidēji ceļš no baltās uz zilo jostu no baltās jo tas aizņem vismaz 2 gadus un 4 cikla atkārtojumus.

  • Week 1

    • Overview of the basic positions: Closed Guard, Half Guard, Side Control, Knee on Belly, Back Control, North/South, Controlling Top Position.

  • Week 2

    • Double leg takedown
    • Armbar from the Guard
    • Opening the Closed Guard

  • Week 3

    • Single Leg Takedown (near side)
    • Triangle Choke from Closed Guard
    • Cross Body to Mount Transition

  • Week 4

    • Single Leg Takedown (far side)
    • Omoplata from Closed Guard
    • Half guard pass

  • Week 5

    • Hip Throw
    • Kimura from Closed Guard
    • Upa Escape from the Mount (with the choke defense)

  • Week 6

    • Shoulder Throw
    • Americana (V-Lock) from Cross Body
    • Elbow Escape from the Mount

  • Week 7

    • Sprawl — Defending Leg Shots
    • Armbar from the Mount
    • Shrimp Escape from Cross Body

  • Week 8

    • Defending a Rear Strangle (Standing Position)
    • Armbar Defense from Inside of the Closed Guard
    • Scissor Sweep

  • Week 9

    • Guillotine choke defense (Standing Position)
    • Defending the Armbar (Flat on the Back)
    • Posture inside Closed Guard

  • Week 10

    • Broom Sweep
    • Knee on Belly Escape

  • Week 11

    • Standing Head Lock Defense (Hands Not Connected)
    • Cross Body (Side Control) Positional Control
